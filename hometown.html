Tangerang

Tangerang is a city located in the Banten province of Indonesia. It is part of the Jabodetabek metropolitan area (Jakarta, Bogor, Depok, Tangerang, Bekasi) and serves as a center for industry, commerce, and residential areas around the national capital, Jakarta. Tangerang has a long history that reflects the region's development from the colonial era to the modern day.
